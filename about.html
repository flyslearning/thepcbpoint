About Us

The PCB Point Started With One Mission

To provide NEET aspirants with authentic guidance, honest support, and real personalized mentorship.

The PCB Point started in 2018 as a small Instagram page with one goal — to provide NEET aspirants with authentic guidance and support.

Our founder experienced firsthand how confusing the NEET journey can be without proper mentorship. Determined to help students avoid the same mistakes, he began sharing honest advice and preparation strategies online.

What started as a single page soon grew into a nationwide community. Today, The PCB Point is powered by a team of medical students and mentors from top medical colleges across India, dedicated to helping the next generation of doctors achieve their dreams.

Over the years, our students have secured admissions in AIIMS and other leading medical colleges across India and abroad. Our mentorship has helped students achieve outstanding results, including AIR 287 in NEET 2025.

💚

At The PCB Point, our mission remains simple: to make quality guidance and real personalized mentorship accessible to every NEET aspirant.
